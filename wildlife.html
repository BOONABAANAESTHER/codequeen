WILDLIFE IN UGANDA

Wild Animals Found in Uganda: What you should expect to see on your Africa safari Uganda, the Pearl of Africa, is a place where every nature lover can have a once-in-a-lifetime glimpse of the Africa safari big five wild animals: Lion, Leopard, Elephant, Giraffe, Rhinoceros and the great apes, the mountain gorilla, and chimpanzee. But that’s not all; the country boasts over 350 species of mammals, from the mighty giant mountain gorillas to the smallest ones like bats, bush babies, and shrews. Uganda is also home to over 1k bird species, many reptiles, and herbivores, making it a true paradise for wildlife enthusiasts. In this article, we’ll take a closer look at the various animals found in Uganda.
